About me

I'm an Industrial Electronics & Automatic Control Engineer with an MSc in Robotics and Automatic Control from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). Today I work as an AI Engineer at Escribano Mechanical & Engineering, building real-time tracking and deep learning perception for autonomous robotic systems.

My path runs through autonomous racing, agricultural robots, astrophysics instrumentation and award-winning hackathons. The common thread: turning cameras, sensors and neural networks into systems that perceive and act in the real world.
