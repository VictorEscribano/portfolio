Overview

Overall winners of HackUPC 2023. Deep Tour is a Blender plugin that delivers a full 360º VR walkthrough of real-estate properties and lets users restyle any room on the fly.

Furniture is identified with Restb.ai and the Segment Anything Model (SAM), and rooms are re-imagined using Stable Diffusion in-painting — all optimized to run on a standard laptop. My contribution fused the segmentation map of the HDRI panorama with the Restb.ai centroids and the spherical-coordinate projection that drives the VR experience.

Built in under 36 hours with a team of four.

Highlights

  • 360º VR property tours running inside Blender
  • On-the-fly room restyling via Stable Diffusion in-painting
  • Furniture identification with SAM + Restb.ai + DINOv2
  • Runs on standard laptops · built in <36h · 🏆 1st place
